About Us

Our mission, vision and how we help injured animals

Our Mission

At Paw Patrol, we are dedicated to connecting injured animals with nearby NGOs for immediate rescue and care. Our platform enables quick response times and efficient communication between concerned citizens and animal welfare organizations. With advanced AI-powered detection, we aim to reduce the suffering of injured animals and provide them with the care they need as quickly as possible. Our real-time alert and tracking system ensures that NGOs are notified immediately when a case is in their vicinity.

Why Choose Us?

"When an animal is injured or lost, time is of the essence. Traditional methods of seeking help often involve a frantic and time-consuming process of calling multiple shelters, NGOs, and veterinary clinics, with no guarantee of immediate assistance. You might find yourself making numerous calls, facing unanswered lines, or struggling to connect with the right resources in your vicinity. This delay can be critical, impacting the animal's well-being and chances of recovery. Our platform offers a revolutionary alternative. We understand the urgency and frustration of these traditional methods, and that's why we've built a smarter, faster, and more reliable solution. With our intuitive system, you simply upload an image of the animal in need. Our advanced AI instantly verifies the situation and immediately alerts a network of verified animal welfare organizations and veterinary professionals in the animal's precise location.
